About my future

I always feel insecure and stressed when I start thinking about and planning my future because I am not as good as others who are the same age. The movie “Get a Job”, a script by Anna Kendrick impressed me so much. She said, “I'm 22 years old and I have no idea what I'm going to do with the rest of my life.”

Every time I think about the future, I feel confused, but I am not fine with that. I just know that my future is my own.

Future career

In the future, I want to work in the media and communication industry, like in a public relations agency, a magazine company or a radio station, etc. I like to communicate with others, I want to work in an industry that could let me build relationships and connections with people.

In Hong Kong, I studied Journalism in College of International Education in Hong Kong.I learnt how to broadcast and I found it fun and interesting, so I am quite interested in the broadcast industry, I want to work in a television station, either speaking in front of the TV or working as a behind-scenes crew.

I want to cover different types of stories, from entertainment to society, even politics, I hope my story can bring reflection to people.

Slasher

Sometimes I think about being a slasher because I want to try multiple careers at the same time, for example, I want to be a content creator like a YouTuber, meanwhile I am a broadcasting journalist. Although it is challenging to do different jobs, it is still interesting.

Traveller

I like travelling. I like to experience different cultures all around the world, meet new people from different countries, and see a lot of beautiful natural views. I hope I can travel to Maldives and Iceland with my family one day in the future because it is beautiful and I like the sunshine, sky and sea.

The countries that I want to travel

  1. Maldives
  2. Iceland
  3. France
