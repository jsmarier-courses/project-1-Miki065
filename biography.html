My hobbies

I have many hobbies, such as video making, taking pictures, cooking, listening to music and singing. In all these hobbies, I like video making the most because I like sharing.

Little beginning of filming

I started to be interested in video making in 2020 mainly because I spent more time staying at home due to the Covid. I tried to vlog my daily life with my family, although my filming skills and editing skills were not very mature at that time, I found it fun and challenging. In December 2022, I started running my own YouTube channel despite setting my video as unlisted just because I was too shy to publicise my video and let people know.

You might confused about why I am too shy to publicise my video and let people know I run a YouTube Channel. In Asia, people always tease the YouTubers and judge them. That is why I was scared to let people, even my friends, know I posted my video on YouTube.
